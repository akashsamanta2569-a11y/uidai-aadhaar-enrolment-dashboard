Work Function and Purpose of data.gov.in

data.gov.in is the Government of India's open-data platform designed to make government datasets publicly available for transparency, innovation, and informed decision-making. It bridges the gap between government data and citizens.

Key Data Insight

The data highlights a demographic shift: New Aadhaar enrolments in top states are dominated by the 0-5 and 5-17 age groups. This suggests near-100% coverage among adults, shifting the focus of enrolment centers to child updates and newborn registration.
